Who We Are

butterfly effect [noun]: a property of chaotic systems (such as the atmosphere) by which small changes in initial conditions can lead to large-scale variations in the future state of the system.

The Butterfly Effect is a project aimed at helping those who are experiencing homeless, or those who would like to help, find the resources that they need in order to make greater and better changes for their futures. We believe that even the smallest changes in one's everyday life can have a positive impact on the future of not only the individual, but all of those around them.
