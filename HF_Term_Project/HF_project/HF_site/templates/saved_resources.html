How YOU Can Help End Homelessness

While the causes and solutions to homelessness are extemely complex, there is still so much that an individual or community can do to help. Regardless of your skills, interests, age, or resources, there are ways that you can make a difference for those who are experiencing homelessness. Volunteer work, advocacy efforts, contributions, and continued self-education are all vital as we work towards finding a solution. Some suggestions are listed below.

Accessing Food

Shelters typically provide food to those who are experiencing homelessness. Find a shelter near you.
